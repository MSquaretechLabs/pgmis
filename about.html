About Pushkar Ganesh

Pushkar Ganesh Cotton Club Export Private limited is recognized by the Government of India. The Company was established in year 1996 by Mr. Mohan Choudhary who was later joint by his three brothers- Mr. Manaram Choudhary, Mr. Magilal Choudhary and Mr. Ganesh ram Choudhary for further assistance. These four gentlemen together add an industrial experience of 20 years to the company. The Company is engaged in the manufacturing and exporting of Hi Fashion ladies garments, Dresses, Tunic, Top, Skirts, T-Shirts, Trousers, Jackets, Scarves, and Bags etc. The Company is also specialized in hand work, embroidery, related work. The company engaged towards 100% export to France, Spain, UK, USA, Italy, Australia, etc.
